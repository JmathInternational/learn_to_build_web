STUDENT PORTAL

Student Portal is speically designed for students, which includes mathematics learning materials (video lessons, exercised, and games) and a forum for students to post questions.

TEACHER PORTAL

Teacher Portal is created for mathematics teachers who looks for teaching materials such as lesson plans, textbooks, teacher manuals and exams. In addition, Teacher Portal provides a forum for teachers to share their ides on teaching and tips on keeping the life balance, and to organize international conference and local social events.

RESEARCH PORTAL

Researcher Portalis catered for mathematics education researchers who desire to keep updated with world leading researcheres and teaching practices in the world. Similar to Student Portal and Teacher Portal, the Researcher Portal also provides a forum for researchers to look out for potential collaborating coworkers, schools and students.
